Dekalb County


Dekalb County recently went through a school redistricting process affecting students all the way from elementary to high school level. The original plan put forth by the county would have divided up the county based on the simple logistics of each area but did not take into account a lot of local concerns. The residents successfully banded together to collect data and come with an alternate plan that suited everyone's requirements and successfully put forth a proposal to the school board
