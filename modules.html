Redistricting Modules Portal

These steps are designed to guide you through the process of preparing an effective redistricting proposal for your school district. Use the navigation pane on the left to aceess details and resources for each step

Goal Formation and Clarification

It is important to set clear goals for your endeavor, begin by thinking anout why you are dissatisfied with the current redistricting plan and what are the key things you would like to change about it. You may want to talk to your neighbours and arrive at a common set of goals for your neighbourhood. Some possible goals might be: Making sure the school is within a certain radius from your home or that the school has a good science lab

Current Model Analysis

Before you can begin to devise an alternative it is important that you have a strong understanding of the current plan. The best way to do this is collecting all the available documents from your school board, this is usually possible through your district board's website.....

Data Collection

Data collection is one of the most crucial and time consuming part of this process. Begin by perusing some of the templates we have prepared to aid you in this process. These templates are meant to serve as a way to help you decide what sort of data you will need to come up with and present a strong case for your proposal
